    Ansible Collaborative

    For queries and assistance related to the Ansible® user ecosystem:

    • Find a public working group that matches your interests.
    • Attend or organize an Ansible user event.
    • Submit news or contribute to the Bullhorn newsletter.
    • Get help by asking questions directly to users of Ansible.

    If you still have questions or need help, you can get in touch with the Ansible Catalyst team at Red Hat® by emailing ansible-catalyst@redhat.com
