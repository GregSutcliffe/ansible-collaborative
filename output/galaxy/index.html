    Ansible Galaxy

    Galaxy is the hub where the Ansible community shares automation content.

    Ansible Galaxy helps you get started automating. Download and plug roles and collections into your playbooks, then watch them go to work. As you iterate over your playbooks and improve them, Ansible Galaxy helps you simplify and streamline your automation. Discover thousands of automation solutions built by people who use Ansible every day and learn from other Ansible users and contributors. Finally, you can import your own roles and collections to make them available to the entire Ansible community.
